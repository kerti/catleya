How To Use

Switches

This section will allow you to turn a device on or off. To turn a device on or off, just hit the switch next to it. Devices that are under timer control are disabled, and you can go to the timers section to set when those devices should turn on or off.

Timers

This section will allow you to set timers to automaticall turn on or turn off your devices. This is useful for example when you want your garden lamp to turn on at dusk and turn off at dawn automatically.

Devices

Thi section will allow you to assign your Raspberry Pi's GPIO pins to devices, give them names, and setup their default settings. You don't have to use all 16 pins, just the ones you need.
